ℹ️ How to use NightWatch

NightWatch helps you decide what to watch in seconds instead of endless scrolling. Here's the quick version:

  1. Pick your inputs — a mood, how much time you have, and/or a genre. Or skip it all and hit 🎲 Surprise me.
  2. Browse the suggestions — each card shows the poster, year, rating, a short synopsis, and where it's streaming in your region.
  3. Save or dismiss — tap ♥ to keep something for later (find it in Favorites), or ✕ to stop it showing up again.
  4. Want more? — hit Show me more to re-roll a fresh batch with the same inputs.

Two ways to run it: use Demo mode for instant sample titles (no setup), or add a free TMDb API key (via the ⚙︎ settings button) for live data and real streaming availability. Your key and your favorites stay only in this browser — no account, nothing sent anywhere else.

One-time setup

NightWatch uses the free public TMDb API for movie/TV data and streaming availability. Paste your free API key (v3 auth) to get started. It is stored only in your browser.
